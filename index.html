About MindBridge

Our mission is to make mental wellness support accessible to everyone, anywhere, anytime.

MindBridge was created by a team of undergraduate computer science students passionate about leveraging technology to support mental well-being. While we are not mental health professionals, we have collaborated with experts and individuals with lived experiences to build a platform that offers accessible and compassionate support.

We recognize that traditional mental health services can be challenging to access due to cost, stigma, or availability. That’s why we developed MindBridge—to provide continuous, technology-driven support while prioritizing ethical and thoughtful design.

Although MindBridge is not a substitute for professional therapy, it serves as a valuable tool for daily mental wellness and can help connect users with professional resources when needed.
